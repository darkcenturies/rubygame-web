Milestone: the economy update is complete. R47 reworked what things sell for; this release finishes the other half — every buy price re-anchored to measured income. 398 listings rise, nothing was lowered, and no sell price changed.

  • Buy prices were calibrated when top income was believed to be ~3,000/hr; measured income is ~7,400/hr, so the whole catalogue had become 2.5–5× cheaper in real playing time than intended.
  • Four mined materials rise to their measured gathering value: coal 4.80 → 15.03, redstone 3.20 → 6.16, lapis 6.60 → 11.45, obsidian 1.03 → 8.02. Crafted items follow their ingredients (torches, lamps, redstone devices), and storage blocks cost exactly 9× their material, so the block form can never undercut a raise.
  • Food is priced on the same scale as everything else: the nutrition coefficient rises from 0.25 to 0.60 (bread 2.75 → 6.60, cooked beef 5.20 → 12.48). The pantry is balanced, not subsidized.
  • Bulk stone can no longer be sold: granite, diorite, andesite, tuff, calcite, deepslate, soul soil and end stone, plus their 34 cut and polished forms. TNT drops 100% of its loot since 1.14, so blast mining produces these in unlimited volume — the same rule that already covers cobblestone. End stone was the real leak: ~15,000 blocks/hr of risk-free end busting at 0.16 paid ~2,400/hr. Masons remain the intended buyer for bulk stone.
  • Versioning note: letters are back. The line ran 0.1a–0.1k in June, the 0.1l-RC series was absorbed into R1 by the old promotion rule, and R1–R48 were letterless revisions. Milestone releases carry letters from here on; -R revisions attach to them.

What's different from vanilla

Gameplay

  • Craftable god applesEnchanted golden apples can be crafted again: 8 gold blocks around an apple.
  • Dragon dropsEvery ender dragon kill drops an egg and an elytra, so the fight stays worth repeating.
  • Bats drop membranesPhantom membranes come from bats too — elytra repair without waiting for phantoms.
  • Silence mobsName a mob “Silence Me” to mute it. Perfect for farms and pets.
  • Storm channelingChanneling tridents work in any weather, not only during thunderstorms.
  • Smooth night skippingSleeping fast-forwards the night smoothly instead of snapping to morning.
  • All recipes unlockedYour recipe book is fully unlocked on first join — no hunting for unlocks.
  • No anvil cost limitThe “too expensive” anvil wall is gone; gear can always be repaired and enchanted.

Quality of life

  • Item finder/finditem searches nearby containers and points you at what you are looking for.
  • Double doorsDouble doors open and close together.
  • Better harvestingRight-click a mature crop to harvest and replant it in one action.
  • Chest sortingSort any container from its inventory screen.
  • Invisible item framesItem frames can be made invisible for clean builds.
  • Player heads/heads gives access to a large decorative head library.
  • XP bottlingBottle your experience and carry it with you.

Looks

  • Round sun and moonThe sun and every moon phase are round instead of square.
  • Twinkling starsThe night sky shimmers — the stars gently twinkle instead of sitting static.
  • Subtle rain and snowVanilla Tweaks unobtrusive weather — rain and snow no longer wash out the view.
  • Poppies are rosesPoppies render as roses again, the way they did before 1.7.
  • Rubies, not emeraldsThe currency is reskinned end to end: ruby ore, ruby blocks and the ruby item.
  • Carved pumpkin facesThe Vanilla Tweaks pumpkin carving contest winners replace the single vanilla face.

New items & enchantments

  • SicklesHarvest a whole area of crops in one swing, in every tool tier.
  • Backpacks & pouchesPortable storage crafted from a shulker box or leather.
  • ScrollsHome, death and unstable scrolls for getting around. (Spawn and lodestone scrolls are switched off here.)
  • Ancient tomesThree tiers of tome that carry the custom enchantments below.
  • Ten custom enchantmentsAngel, Grappling Hook, Hell Bent, Leafchopper, Rake, Seeding, Soulbound, Take Off, Unbreakable and Wings.
  • Handy toolsTrowel, file, north compass, papyrus scroll, slime bucket and a reinforced elytra.
